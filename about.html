Data Engineering Built on Operational Expertise

Foundations: Where Data Meets Real-World Impact

I grew up in San Cristobal, Venezuela and earned a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Florida, with a focus on instrumentation and measurements. At the University's Hurricane Research center, I learned a critical lesson: data collection only matters when it enables real decisions.

Working on hurricanes Dennis, Katrina, and Wilma gave me real-time data acquisition experience under challenging conditions. I learned that even the best instruments are worthless without actionable insights. That lesson shaped my entire approach to data engineering.

Global Operations: Understanding Business from the Ground Up

As a Field Engineer at Schlumberger in Oman and Venezuela, I managed drilling operations and integrated projects. This gave me a practical understanding of how operational decisions affect business outcomes, safety, and profitability.

In Canada, I shifted to Quality Control and Assurance in oil and gas manufacturing. As a Quality Specialist for six years, I focused on understanding why systems fail and how to prevent failures. This experience developed my approach to root cause analysis and continuous improvement, which I now apply to every data solution I build.

This operational experience gave me a business-first perspective. I understand what matters to operations managers and executives, and how to translate technical problems into business outcomes.

The Bridge: From Operations to Senior Data Engineering

My move into IT was a natural progression. I'd seen how operational inefficiencies cost companies millions and how data insights can prevent problems. I wanted to build technology solutions that address real business needs.

Starting in application development and automation in financial services, my operational background proved valuable. I understood not just how to build solutions, but what problems they needed to solve and how to measure success in terms of ROI and operational impact.

Over five years as a data engineer, I've applied this approach across oil and gas, finance, and manufacturing. I've reduced warehouse costs by 30%, accelerated ETL processes by 75%, and improved incident tracking. My focus is on delivering measurable business value while exploring emerging technologies.

As a Senior Data Engineer at a leading financial institution, I combine operational insight with modern data engineering to build solutions that drive measurable business outcomes.
