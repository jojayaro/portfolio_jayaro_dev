Engineering Approach

  • Low Abstraction First: Prefer simple, explicit code paths that ease debugging and reduce hidden costs.
  • Determinism: Idempotent ingestion and schema aware transforms for predictable recovery and repeatability.
  • Observability Built In: Timers, counts, and outcome signals embedded early rather than bolted on.
  • Cost Awareness: Track resource usage and align optimization to business value, not benchmarks alone.
  • Incremental Delivery: Ship thin vertical slices (ingest to insight) to validate value before scaling.
  • Resilience Over Cleverness: Favor clear fallbacks and safe retries to reduce incident time to isolation.
